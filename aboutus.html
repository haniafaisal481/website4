About HOiD – Modern Living in Affordable Prices
who we are

HOiD celebrated it’s 10 years in business anniversary in April 2023 – marks a decade of service excellence. We celebrate 10 years of serving customers with exceptional home and office furnishing items. A big thank you to all our valued customers who showed great faith and trust in us. It is because of all your trust and support that this 10 year journey has culminated in such a huge success.We are excited to continue our commitment to excellence and to embark on the journey ahead, bringing you the finest furniture for many more years to come. Thank you for being a part of our story and for making us your destination for all your furniture needs. HOiD Started as Pakistan’s first online furniture store that has been in business since 2013 with the goal of making modern design more accessible. With over a year in business, HOiD has established a lot by offering a wide-range of well designed, functional home and office furnishing products. It has been an established and popular company with an excellent track record for best customer satisfaction. HOiD has never compromised on the quality and services provided to their customers. Customer satisfaction is our top priority, we believe in keeping the customers happy and providing them with products at excellent prices. Always on the look-out, we are constantly growing our catalog to offer you a wealth of fresh ideas and new finds each time you visit. We offer something for every design enthusiast, whether you’re making over an entire space or refreshing the details. Every day, we work to enable you to live life more fully through design and to help you through each phase of your own style evolution. Thanks for letting us be part of your journey!
